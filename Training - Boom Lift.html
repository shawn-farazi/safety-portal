Certification valid 3 years · 80% to pass the quiz · ANSI A92.22 / OSHA aerial lift

2 Send the quiz to the employee

After the video, copy the quiz link and send it to the employee in Microsoft Teams (or text/email). They open it on their phone — no sign-in needed — and must score 80%+ to pass.

Open Quiz ✓ Copied — paste it into Teams

3 Practical evaluation

Once the employee passes the quiz, schedule the hands-on practical evaluation with a qualified trainer. They must pass 100% of the checklist to be fully certified, then a certification card is generated.
